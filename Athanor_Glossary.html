Every term,
every citation

The whole toolkit runs on two kinds of number: code-derived values — traceable to a clause of the NCC or an Australian Standard, which you cite and verify — and named conventions — industry rules of thumb with a source but no legal status, which you attribute and flag. This page collects every code, standard, regulation, reference and acronym the five tools draw on, so you can find the source behind any figure and quote it in your documentation.

All NCC references are NCC 2022 Volume One, as adopted in South Australia (NCC 2025 is deferred in SA until 1 May 2027). Clause numbers are a guide — verify the current clause text yourself at ncc.abcb.gov.au before it appears in a submission. Standards are cited by number; check you are using the current edition.
